Weapon Editor:
To access the weapon editor, navigate to Content/EditorUtilityWidget. Find "WeaponEditor" within this folder, right click it and select "Run Editor Utility Widget".

Prototypes:
To access the prototypes, navigate to Content/Prototypes within the project. This folder holds two levels: DS_Prototype and WAR_Prototype. These are weapon ranges with a few weapons created in different styles to test on test dummies.
